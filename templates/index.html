Welcome to Southern Connections!

Unique to Southern Connecticut State University, Southern Connections allows our students a way to connect to others within their major creating lasting connections. Partnering with Linkedin, it is our hope that those connections continue long after our students graduate.

{% if current_user.is_anonymous: %} {% endif %}

Majors

A group exists for every major and upon signup, new users are added to their major group so they can start networking right away! For every group, there are events hosted by the university as a way to get to know your peers, ask questions to those who may know from experience, and foster the best possible college experience!

Meetups

Once you have an account with us, check out the meetups available for your major group, as well as others! Join any meetups of interest and those of other majors to see what those groups are all about!

Linkedin

Any account with us requires a Linkedin account to keep your lasting connections long after you've graduated Southern! We pull some basic information from your Linkedin profile to make setting up your profile with us that much easier. Be sure to keep your Linkedin information up to date for the best possible experience!
